# Damage related technical aspects

## Knockback Formula

### In other PFs

|||
|-|-|
| p | percentage of the target (after damage application) |
| d | attack damage |
| w | target weight |
| s | scaling knockback of the attack |
| b | base knockback of the attack |
| r | other ratios |
|||
